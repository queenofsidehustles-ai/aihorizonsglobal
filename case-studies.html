Case Study 01
Dazzle & Shine Maids
Home Services · Residential & Airbnb Cleaning · Orlando, FL
Operational system · CRM & payments · SMS automation · Workforce & payouts

A residential and Airbnb cleaning business rebuilt from the ground up — replacing spreadsheets and disconnected tools with one integrated operating system that runs bookings, payments, communication, hiring, and contractor payouts.

The problem

Like most home-service businesses, Dazzle & Shine ran on spreadsheets, sticky notes, and disconnected apps that were never built for a cleaning operation — creating friction from the first inquiry to the moment a cleaner showed up at the door.

  • No single pipeline to track a lead from inquiry to booked job
  • Manual, slow screening and hiring of reliable cleaners
  • Inconsistent communication with customers and the team
  • No clean way for cleaners to claim and manage jobs
  • Informal pricing with no standard/deep/move-out tiers
  • After-hours leads slipped away with no coverage

What was built

  • Integrated CRM — leads, quotes, bookings, and customer history in one live system, deployed on Railway
  • Two-way text inbox — automated SMS + email (Twilio & Resend), with instant owner alerts so no message is missed
  • Customer payments made easy — pay-by-link, morning-of auto-invoices, and an on-site QR "Take Payment" option
  • "Offer to the team" job board — open jobs are broadcast and the first available cleaner claims them (Uber-style), with time-clash protection
  • Contractor payouts — automated splits via Stripe Connect, with each cleaner onboarded to receive their own payments
  • Guided 7-step job screen — a provider playbook that keeps quality consistent on every clean
  • Async video-interview screening for faster, standardized hiring
  • Three-tier pricing (standard, deep, move-in/move-out) that protects margins and reduces scope creep
  • Ratings, reviews & Google-review capture, plus an installable home-screen app (PWA)

Case Study 02
Government of Ghana
Public Sector · National AI Strategy · West Africa
Government consulting · AI strategy · National policy · Ministry implementation

A comprehensive AI-strategy proposal package developed in alignment with Ghana's National AI Strategy 2025–2035 — reframing the country's execution gap as a live national risk and giving leaders a practical path from vision to action.

The challenge

Ghana's national strategy set the vision. What was missing was the execution framework — the ministry-level guidance to turn policy into operational reality. Without it, the strategy risked becoming another document that looks good on paper but doesn't hold.

What was delivered

  • A full 8-page repositioned proposal built around Ghana's real execution challenges — governance, workforce readiness, infrastructure, and institutional capacity
  • A one-page executive leave-behind for the decision-maker who has 5 minutes, not 50
  • A plain-language version written at ~5th-grade reading level so the strategy communicates at every level of government
  • A Ministry of Food & Agriculture white paper — AI for agricultural data, supply-chain visibility, and food-security monitoring
  • A Ministry of Lands & Natural Resources white paper — AI for land-registry modernization and environmental compliance
